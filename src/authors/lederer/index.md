I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Computational Social Choice group of led by Ulle Endriss at the Institute of Logic, Language, and Computation (ILLC) at the University of Amsterdam. Being a part of the <a href="https://advancingdemocracy.eu/">ADDI</a> project, my research focuses on (computational) social choice with a particular focus on axiomatics and the design of normatively appealing mechanisms for voting, matching, and assignment.
